The story behind the biomes

What is Biomes?

Biomes is a visual atlas of Earth's major ecosystems. From the dense canopies of tropical rainforests to the frozen expanses of polar tundra, we document the extraordinary diversity of life across our planet.

Five Major Ecosystems

We document five major biome categories, each home to unique plant and animal life shaped by climate, geography, and evolution.

By the Numbers

  • 8.7M estimated species on Earth
  • 1M+ species facing extinction
  • 71% of Earth's surface is ocean
  • 50% of species live in rainforests
